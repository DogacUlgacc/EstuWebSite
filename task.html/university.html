About Us

Eskişehir Technical University (ESTU) is a leading public university located in the heart of Eskişehir, a vibrant city renowned for its rich cultural heritage, modern infrastructure, and youthful energy. Established in 2018, ESTU is home to five faculties, two vocational schools, one school, and three institutes, as well as four research and application centers spread across three campuses. At ESTU, we strive to provide our students with the most dynamic, innovative, and well-rounded educational experience possible. Our highly qualified teaching staff work tirelessly to ensure that our students are equipped with the skills and knowledge they need to succeed in a rapidly evolving global landscape. With a diverse student body of approximately 9,000 students from all over Turkey and abroad, ESTU is a truly international institution that fosters an inclusive, collaborative, and intellectually stimulating environment. We are proud to be a key player in shaping the future of higher education in Turkey and beyond, and we look forward to continuing to build a world-class institution that prepares our students for the challenges and opportunities of tomorrow.

Numbers of Students and Staff

Eskişehir Technical University, as of the 2021-2022 academic year, has approximately 9,000 students enrolled in its undergraduate and graduate programs. Our university takes pride in having students from all over Turkey, as well as international students from various countries. We have a diverse faculty consisting of over 450 highly qualified and experienced academics, including 169 professors, 76 associate professors, and 178 assistant professors. In addition to our academic staff, we have over 150 administrative and support staff members who work diligently to ensure the smooth operation of our university. Our faculty members are dedicated to providing students with a challenging yet supportive learning environment, while our administrative and support staff provide the necessary services and resources for students to achieve their academic goals. We believe that our students and staff are the cornerstone of our university, and we strive to provide them with the best educational and professional experience possible.

Administrative Units

Eskişehir Technical University has a well-organized administrative structure that is essential for the success of its students, faculty, and staff. The university is comprised of various administrative units that work together to ensure the efficient operation of the institution. These units include the Rectorate, Faculties, Graduate Schools, Research Centers, and Administrative Departments. The Rectorate is responsible for the overall management and leadership of the university, while the Faculties and Graduate Schools focus on academic programs and research activities. The Research Centers focus on interdisciplinary research projects that have the potential to make a real-world impact. The Administrative Departments provide support services such as finance, human resources, and IT. With a competent and experienced team of administrators and staff, Eskişehir Technical University is dedicated to providing its students with the best possible educational experience and contributing to the advancement of science and technology through innovative research and development projects.
